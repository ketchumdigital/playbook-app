Gather Facts

Use the list of questions below to gather critical details about the situation.

The answers to these questions combined with the Issues Triggers Checklist will inform the communications strategy to be deployed by determining the severity of the situation and what information is still unknown. These questions also are listed under potential scenarios.

What Happened?
  • What is the situation/accusation? What is the source of the information? Has it been confirmed?
  • When/where did this take place? At a California dairy?
 What is the Severity of the Situation?
  • Who is affected/involved? Any producers, cows? Is anyone injured or dead?
  • Are there any protests? Who is involved/organizing? What are their demands/calls to action?
  • Has there been activity on social media? By whom (individuals, groups or both)? What is the perceived level of influence/involvement of these commenters?
 What Is the connection/impact to the California dairy industry?
  • Are any California dairies involved? Which ones/how many?
  • Are California dairies the focus of criticism? What is the source of the discussion/criticism? Where is this conversation taking place? Is it growing?
